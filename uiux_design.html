This course is a complete journey into the world of UI and UX Design, created for aspiring designers and beginners who want to build creative and user-friendly digital products. Learn to design engaging interfaces and smooth user experiences for websites, apps, and software. At Artmount Academy Chennai, our expert mentors guide you through real-world projects, helping you master the principles of design thinking, usability, and interaction design with hands-on learning.

What You'll Gain

    By completing this UI/UX Design course in Chennai, you’ll gain a strong foundation in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and interface design. You’ll learn how to understand user behavior, design intuitive layouts, and create prototypes that solve real-world problems. With our project-based approach, you’ll not only learn design tools but also build a professional portfolio that showcases your creativity and design process to employers and clients.

Course Coverage - UI & UX

    Our User Interface and User Experience Design modules cover the full design lifecycle from concept to final prototype. You’ll learn UX research, user journey mapping, information architecture, wireframes, UI layout design, prototyping, and usability testing. Every session helps you think like a designer. Balancing functionality, visual appeal, and user satisfaction in every product you create

Tools Covered

    At Artmount Academy, we train you in industry-standard design tools used by professionals worldwide. You’ll get hands-on experience with Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Miro. You’ll also learn how to use collaboration tools and create responsive designs for web and mobile interfaces. Our practical sessions help you become confident in translating ideas into interactive prototypes.

What Jobs Can You Get?

    After completing the UI/UX course at Artmount Academy Chennai, you’ll be ready to start your career as a UI Designer, UX Designer, Product Designer, Interaction Designer, or UX Researcher. You can also work as a freelance designer or collaborate with startups, design studios, and tech companies. With the growing demand for digital products, this course opens up top career opportunities in Chennai and across India.
